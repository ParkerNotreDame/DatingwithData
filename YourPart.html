    Your Part

    The American birth rate is 1.66 live births per woman. A population needs at least 2 children per woman to sustain its size. The current population halving time is 3.75 generations. In other words, America will be half its current size by the time your children perish, if you have any. But you can end it with one date. So arm yourself with data, strap in, and get to dating and procreating.

    Going into the Dating Environment

    It was the daters who seemed to care just the right amount that performed the best. We suspect that those who wanted a relationship to come out of speed dating might have come off as overbearing, while the ones who just wanted to "say I did it" didn't try hard enough. Those looking just to meet and to have some fun were the most productive.
